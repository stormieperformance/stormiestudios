Masters Gym

Kickboxing & Muay Thai club · Kungsholmen, Stockholm

Where this stands: the technical build below is live and working today, real CMS, real code, real client meetings. The gym's own photography and hero video are still being finalized on their end, so the visual polish on the live site isn't final yet.

The challenge

A dated Squarespace build with no bilingual support, no clear path from visitor to member, and nothing for corporate inquiries. Editable in theory, but content still went stale, the instructor page had placeholder text live for a long stretch regardless.

The approach

A fully custom, hand-coded, bilingual build structured around conversion, with a content system matched to how the club actually works day to day.

i.

Built for conversion

Separate paths for members, families, and corporate training, each with its own call to action, rather than one generic contact form.

ii.

Self-service news

Moved onto a real headless CMS with photo support. Published and confirmed live end to end, Studio to site, no developer required.

iii.

Schedule that fits how they work

Kept on a spreadsheet on purpose, a deliberate call, not a shortcut. It's genuinely the fastest way to edit fast-moving weekly class times.

iv.

Social-first design

A live Instagram feed built in, meeting members where they already look first, which matters a lot in the fitness industry.

Outcome so far
  • Clear conversion paths for individual members and business customers alike
  • News fully self-service, confirmed working with a real published post
  • No placeholder or unfinished content anywhere on the site
  • External CMS and spreadsheet content sanitized before it ever reaches the page
  • Keyboard and screen-reader accessible: modal, toggles, and carousels all operable without a mouse

What's actually under the hood

  • Built by hand, not assembled from a template, so it's the site you'd actually ask for, not a generic layout with a logo swapped in
  • A real content system behind the news section (Sanity), with required fields and photo cropping built in, so it's hard to accidentally publish something broken
  • The class schedule lives in a spreadsheet on purpose, it's simply the fastest way to update, with checks in place so a typo can't break the whole page
  • Anything pulled in from the CMS or spreadsheet is checked before it's shown, so bad or malicious data can't slip onto the page
  • Every page works in Swedish and English from one system, not two separate sites to maintain
  • Membership signups connect straight through to GymControl, no extra steps
  • Fully usable with just a keyboard, and readable by screen readers, not every small studio site bothers with this
  • Set up to actually get found on Google: structured business data, a sitemap, and a proper 404 page instead of a broken one
  • Wired to track real signups and inquiries, not just page views, once the client connects their own analytics account
  • Hosted reliably on GitHub Pages; the real domain connection is in progress
  • Light and dark mode, fully responsive, built mobile-first
Also true right now: the contact form's backend isn't wired up yet, that's waiting on the client's own form-handling account, in keeping with the same principle as everything else here: their infrastructure, their ownership, not mine.
