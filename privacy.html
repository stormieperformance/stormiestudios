Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

Data I collect

When you use a contact or application form on this site, I collect the information you provide: your name, email address, and any message you send.

Purpose

This information is used only to respond to your enquiry, whether that's a project request or an application for a free Giving Back website.

Legal basis

This processing is based on legitimate interest under GDPR, since I need to be able to respond to enquiries I receive.

Storage

Submissions are received and stored by Formspree, the third-party service that delivers form messages to my inbox. They are normally deleted within 12 months of the enquiry being resolved.

Sharing of data

Your information is not shared with any third party, other than Formspree, which is used to receive and deliver form submissions.

Your rights

You have the right to: request information about what data is stored about you, request correction or deletion of your data, and object to the processing. You can also file a complaint with the Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection (IMY). I aim to respond to such requests within one month.
