Giving Back

Free websites for

welfare organizations.

I've loved animals for as long as I can remember, and giving back to the people who dedicate their lives to protecting them feels like the right place to start.

As Stormie Studios launches, I'm offering 2-3 custom websites completely free each year to registered non-profit organizations working in animal rescue, wildlife conservation, or animal welfare.

Every website is designed and built by me from scratch, so I can only take on a small number of projects. If your organization could benefit from a new website, I'd love to hear from you.
