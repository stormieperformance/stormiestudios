How it works

Three steps. No surprises.

1

We talk

Every club, coach and business is different. We start by understanding your goals, your clients and the role the website should play in the business.

2

Built together

Whenever possible, I review your existing website, social media and public material before we meet. That gives us something concrete to discuss from the start. From there, we test ideas, make adjustments and keep improving the site together.

3

Live on your domain

The site launches on your address with forms, booking and everything connected. From day one you update schedules, news and prices yourself, and I handle the operations behind the scenes.

Easy for you.
Handled by me.

Your content is always yours. Depending on the project, you update it yourself in a spreadsheet or through a clean CMS dashboard built for non-developers. No manuals, no agency middleman.

Everything you don't want to think about, like hosting, security, backups and updates, I handle for a flat monthly fee. Less than what a template platform charges, with none of the limitations.

Faq

Common questions.

How long does a project take?+

Most projects launch within a few weeks of our first call, depending on scope and how quickly content comes together.

Do you provide hosting?+

Yes, hosting, security, backups and updates are handled for a flat monthly fee, or you're welcome to bring your own.

Can you use my existing domain?+

Yes, I connect your existing domain and set up HTTPS as part of the build.

Can I update the website myself?+

Yes, most content, schedules, prices and news, is editable by you directly, no developer needed.

Do you work outside Stockholm?+

Absolutely. Most of my clients are here in Stockholm today, but I work just as easily with clients anywhere else, over video calls and email.

Why not just use an AI website builder?+

An AI builder can generate a page in minutes, but it doesn't know how your gym actually runs. I build the schedule, membership paths and content system around your real workflow, not a generic template, and I'm a person you can reach directly when something needs to change or something breaks.
