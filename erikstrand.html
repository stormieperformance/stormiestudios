Erik Strand is a personal trainer in Stockholm. Like most solo coaches, he had the expertise — but not the digital presence to match. No place to be found, no way to sell, no system to book clients.

We built a complete ecosystem. A premium website that positions him as the expert he is. A digital shop that sells training programs without a monthly Shopify fee. And a booking page that removes friction from the first contact.

This is a concept project — built to demonstrate what a complete digital setup looks like for an independent personal trainer. Interested in something similar? Get in touch.
